Smart Banking System with Personalized Credit Recommendations

Motivation of the project

As a dual major in Computer Science and Business Administration, I am passionate about projects that merge technical innovation with business insights. This banking system represents an exciting challenge to develop a platform that not only leverages my technical skills but also applies them to real-world financial scenarios, aiming to streamline and personalize user experiences in banking.

The project is envisioned to support key banking functionalities such as account creation, deposits, withdrawals, and balance inquiries. Moreover, it will feature an innovative credit card recommendation engine tailored to users' financial profiles. At this initial stage, we are laying out the foundational code and structures to support these functionalities.

The preliminary design focuses on creating a robust and scalable framework using object-oriented principles. The choice of Java as the primary programming language is driven by its security features and strong application in financial systems. The design incorporates a clear separation of concerns through the MVC pattern, ensuring that the system is maintainable and adaptable as it evolves.

Currently, I am in the process of setting up the basic modules for account management and beginning to outline the logic for the credit card recommendation feature. My approach includes embracing test-driven development from the start, which will guide the construction of reliable and efficient code. As the project progresses, agile methodologies will be employed to adapt to changing requirements and feedback.
